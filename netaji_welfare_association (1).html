Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2024, Netaji Welfare Association is a passionate community organization rooted in the spirit of unity, empowerment, and wellness. We believe that a strong community is built on the pillars of health, youth engagement, and collective action.

Our flagship annual Marathon Event brings together participants from across the region to celebrate fitness, determination, and community spirit. We are committed to organizing impactful social events that leave a lasting positive mark.

Under the tagline "Rise and Roar", we inspire every individual to push their limits and contribute to a stronger, healthier society.
